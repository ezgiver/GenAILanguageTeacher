# Loqua Language Learning Platform
We are online Language Learning Platform and want to intagrate Gen AI into our Product.

## Business Problem

Many learners hesitate to practice speaking because they fear making mistakes or being judged by teachers or classmates. As a result, they avoid speaking practice and progress more slowly.


## Business Use Case

**As a** Loqua student,  
**I want** to learn French with an AI teacher,  
**so that** I can practice speaking confidently without feeling embarrassed or judged.

## Vision

Start with French and gradually expand to other European languages using the same AI-powered learning experience.
